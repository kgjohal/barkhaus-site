About Barkhaus

Barkhaus is a community-first dog social club — a place where humans and pups alike can connect, unwind, and enjoy curated experiences. Whether you're grabbing a drink, attending an event, or just socializing, Barkhaus is your dog's new favorite place (and yours too).

Membership

We offer flexible plans for every kind of pup parent. Enjoy unlimited access, exclusive member events, and perks from our partners. Join the Barkhaus family today.

Events

From puppy yoga to dog-friendly movie nights, Barkhaus events are designed to bring the community together. Check back regularly or follow us on Instagram for updates.
